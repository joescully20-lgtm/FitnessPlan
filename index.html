Why this works for you

At 44, testosterone and metabolism are shifting — but this is absolutely not a disadvantage if you train smart. Resistance training will be your #1 fat-loss tool (more effective than cardio at your age). We're pairing it with a meaningful calorie deficit targeting 2 lbs/week — aggressive but achievable with high protein to protect your muscle. The supplement protocol directly targets the cortisol-testosterone axis, which becomes increasingly important in your 40s: high cortisol suppresses testosterone and drives belly fat storage. By managing stress hormones through ashwagandha, magnesium, and zinc while supporting testosterone production, you're stacking your hormonal environment in your favor. The other days, a simple 20–30 min walk after meals is your secret weapon for fat burn and blood sugar control.

The 3 Non-Negotiable Nutrition Rules
1. Hit your protein first. 180g/day is your anchor.
2. Drink 1 gallon of water daily. Most afternoon cravings are dehydration.
3. The 80/20 rule. Eat on plan 80% of the time. Consistency beats perfection.

Discipline System
You said staying motivated, finding time, and eating healthy are your biggest challenges. Here's how we tackle each one head-on.
⏰ FINDING TIME
🗓️
Schedule workouts like meetings
Right now, open your calendar and block 3 sessions this week. 45 minutes each. Label them "Non-negotiable."
⚡
The 10-minute rule
On days you "don't feel like it," commit to just 10 minutes. Almost every time, you'll finish the full session.
🥗 EATING HEALTHY CONSISTENTLY
🍳
Sunday meal prep = weekday wins
Spend 60–90 minutes on Sunday cooking in bulk. When hunger hits during the week, the healthy option is already made.
📱
Use a free tracking app
Download MyFitnessPal or Cronometer. Spend 3 minutes logging meals each day for 4–6 weeks.
🔥 STAYING MOTIVATED
📸
Progress photos every 2 weeks
Same lighting, same time of day, every 2 weeks. Side-by-side photos show the real story.
🏆
Celebrate non-scale victories
The scale will stall. Track other wins: lifted heavier, walked every day, shirt fits looser.
📅
The 30-Day Rule
Commit to this plan for exactly 30 days without evaluating whether it's working. After 30 days you'll have momentum.
Weight Log
Track your weight over time. Log consistently — same time of day works best (morning, after using the bathroom). The trend over weeks matters more than any single number.
